# Sample Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)

## Charity (Mental Health Service) DPIA Example

### 1. Project/Processing Activity
Temporary storage of caller information for mental health support follow-up.

### 2. Purpose of Processing
To enable branch volunteers to contact individuals at an agreed time for support or follow-up.

### 3. Nature of Data Processed
- Name
- Phone number
- Brief description/notes of the call

### 4. Lawful Basis
- Consent (explicitly obtained from caller)
- Legitimate interests (providing support services)

### 5. Data Subjects
- Callers seeking mental/emotional health support

### 6. Risks Identified
- Unauthorised access to sensitive personal data
- Data not deleted after agreed period
- Data breach impacting vulnerable individuals

### 7. Mitigation Measures
- Access restricted to trained staff only
- Data encrypted at rest and in transit
- Automated deletion of data 7 days after call or agreed contact time
- Regular audits of deletion process

### 8. Data Retention
- Data retained for a maximum of 7 days after the call or agreed contact time, then automatically deleted

### 9. Data Sharing
- No sharing with third parties unless required by law or with explicit consent

### 10. Data Subject Rights
- Right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and object

### 11. Consultation
- Data Protection Officer (DPO)
- Call Care support teams

### 12. Review Date
- Annually or upon significant change
