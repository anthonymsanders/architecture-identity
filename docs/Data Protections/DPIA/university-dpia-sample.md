# Sample Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)

## University DPIA Example

### 1. Project/Processing Activity
Implementation of a new student management system to handle admissions, enrolment, and student records.

### 2. Purpose of Processing
To manage student data for academic administration, statutory reporting, and student support.

### 3. Nature of Data Processed
- Name
- Date of birth
- Contact details
- Academic records
- Special category data (e.g., disability, ethnicity)

### 4. Lawful Basis
- Public task (Education Act)
- Consent (where required)

### 5. Data Subjects
- Prospective students
- Current students
- Alumni

### 6. Risks Identified
- Unauthorised access to personal data
- Data loss or corruption
- Inaccurate or outdated records

### 7. Mitigation Measures
- Role-based access controls
- Regular data backups
- Data accuracy checks
- Staff training on data protection

### 8. Data Retention
- Student records retained for statutory periods (e.g., 6 years post-graduation)

### 9. Data Sharing
- Shared with government agencies (e.g., HESA) https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/higher-education-statistical-agency
- Third-party service providers under contract

### 10. Data Subject Rights
- Right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and object

### 11. Consultation
- Data Protection Officer (DPO)
- IT Security
- Student Union (where appropriate)

### 12. Review Date
- Annually or upon significant change

---

*This is a sample DPIA template for educational purposes. Adapt as needed for your institution.*
