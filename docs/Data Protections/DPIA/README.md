# Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) Guidance and Resources

This directory contains sample DPIA forms and supporting information to help organisations in the UK understand and complete DPIAs in line with legal and regulatory requirements.

## DPIA Samples
- [University DPIA Sample](university-dpia-sample.md)
- [Charity (Mental Health Service) DPIA Sample](charity-mental-health-dpia-sample.md)

## Conformance with UK Guidance
The provided DPIA samples follow the structure and content recommended by the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), including:
- Description of the processing activity
- Purpose and lawful basis
- Data subjects and data types
- Risks and mitigation
- Data retention and sharing
- Data subject rights
- Consultation and review

These elements are consistent with the ICO's guidance for DPIAs under the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018.
