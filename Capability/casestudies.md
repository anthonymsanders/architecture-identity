# Case Studies: Identity Products in Use by UK Universities
## OpenText Identity Products
- **Products:** OpenText Identity and Access Management (IAM), Directory Services, Single Sign-On (SSO)
- **Highlights:**
  - Used by some universities for enterprise-grade identity management, directory integration, and SSO.
  - Supports compliance, audit, and governance requirements.
  - Integrates with other platforms (e.g., Microsoft, SAP, Oracle).
- **Reference:** [OpenText IAM Solutions](https://www.opentext.com/products/identity-access-management)
## De Montfort University (DMU)
- **Products:** Microsoft Azure AD, Shibboleth, SSO, MFA
- **Highlights:**
  - Azure AD is used for staff and student authentication and SSO.
  - Shibboleth enables federated access to academic resources.
  - MFA is implemented for enhanced security.
- **Reference:** [DMU IT Services](https://www.dmu.ac.uk/about-dmu/it-and-media-services/index.aspx)

## University of Leicester
- **Products:** Microsoft Azure AD, Shibboleth, SSO, MFA
- **Highlights:**
  - Azure AD provides SSO and identity lifecycle management.
  - Shibboleth is used for federated access to resources.
  - MFA is rolled out for staff and students.
- **Reference:** [University of Leicester IT Services](https://www.le.ac.uk/itservices)

## Coventry University
- **Products:** Microsoft Azure AD, Okta, SSO, MFA
- **Highlights:**
  - Azure AD and Okta are used for SSO and identity management.
  - MFA is required for staff and students.
- **Reference:** [Coventry University IT Services](https://www.coventry.ac.uk/it-services/)

## University of Edinburgh
- **Products:** Shibboleth (SSO), Microsoft Azure AD, Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- **Highlights:**
  - Shibboleth enables federated access to academic resources via the UK Access Management Federation.
  - Azure AD is used for staff identity management and SSO.
  - MFA is required for staff and being rolled out to students.
- **Reference:** [University of Edinburgh - Information Services](https://www.ed.ac.uk/information-services/computing/it-security/identity-management)

## University of Oxford
- **Products:** Shibboleth, Grouper, Microsoft Azure AD, MFA
- **Highlights:**
  - Shibboleth and Grouper support federated identity and group management.
  - Azure AD and MFA provide secure access to university systems.
- **Reference:** [University of Oxford - IT Services](https://www.it.ox.ac.uk/)

## Durham University
- **Products:** Okta, Microsoft Azure AD, SSO, MFA
- **Highlights:**
  - Okta is used for cloud-based SSO and lifecycle management.
  - Azure AD and MFA secure access for staff and students.
- **Reference:** [Durham University - IT Service Desk](https://www.dur.ac.uk/cis/)

## University of Manchester

## University of Glasgow
