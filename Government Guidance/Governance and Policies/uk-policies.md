# UK Policies and Good Practice Guidance for Education

This document outlines key UK policies and best practice guidance relevant to digital identity management for education providers.

---

## 1. Data Protection and Privacy
- **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**: Sets out requirements for processing personal data. [ICO GDPR Guide](https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/guide-to-data-protection/guide-to-the-general-data-protection-regulation-gdpr/)
- **Data Protection Act 2018**: UK-specific data protection law. [DPA 2018 Overview](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2018/12/contents/enacted)
- **ICO Guidance for Education**: [ICO Education Sector Guidance](https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/education/)

## 2. Digital Identity and Trust
- **UK Digital Identity and Attributes Trust Framework**: Sets standards for digital identity providers. [Framework](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/digital-identity-attributes-trust-framework)
- **GOV.UK Verify**: Government digital identity service. [GOV.UK Verify](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/introducing-govuk-verify/introducing-govuk-verify)

## 3. Security and Authentication
- **NCSC Guidance on Identity and Access Management**: [NCSC IAM Collection](https://www.ncsc.gov.uk/collection/identity-and-access-management)
- **Jisc: Security Good Practice for Education**: [Jisc Security](https://www.jisc.ac.uk/guides/security-good-practice-for-education)

## 4. Sector-Specific Good Practice
- **Jisc: Digital Identity Management**: [Jisc Guide](https://www.jisc.ac.uk/guides/digital-identity-management)
- **UCISA: Identity and Access Management Toolkit**: [UCISA Toolkit](https://www.ucisa.ac.uk/Resources/Toolkits/Identity-and-Access-Management)
- **DfE: Data Protection for Schools**: [DfE Guidance](https://www.gov.uk/data-protection-how-we-collect-and-share-research-data)

---

## 5. Summary
Education providers should ensure compliance with UK data protection laws, follow government and sector guidance for digital identity, and implement robust security and privacy practices. Regularly review updates from the ICO, NCSC, Jisc, and UCISA for the latest best practices.
