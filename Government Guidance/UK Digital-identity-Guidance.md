# Digital Identity Good Practice Guidance for Higher Education

## Overview
This document summarizes government and sector agency guidance on digital identity, as well as common identity services used in UK higher education business areas.

## Government and Sector Agencies
- **UK Government (GOV.UK):**
  - [Digital Identity and Attributes Trust Framework](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/digital-identity-and-attributes-trust-framework)
  - [GOV.UK One Login](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/introducing-govuk-one-login/introducing-govuk-one-login)
- **Jisc:**
  - [Digital Identity Management Guidance](https://www.jisc.ac.uk/guides/digital-identity-management)
  - Sector-wide pilots for digital student IDs and verifiable credentials
- **UCISA:**
  - [UCISA Best Practice](https://www.ucisa.ac.uk/Memberships/Membership-benefits)
  - Surveys and events on identity management
- **Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):**
  - [Guide to Data Protection](https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/guide-to-data-protection/)
  - DPIA and compliance guidance

## Good Practice Guidance
- Use federated identity (e.g., SAML, Shibboleth) for secure access to academic resources
- Implement Single Sign-On (SSO) for campus systems
- Adopt Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for staff and students
- Automate provisioning and deprovisioning of accounts
- Enforce regular access reviews and compliance checks
- Issue digital student IDs and verifiable credentials
- Ensure privacy and data protection compliance (UK GDPR, Data Protection Act)

## Typical Identity Services in Business Areas
| Business Area   | Identity Services Used                  |
|----------------|-----------------------------------------|
| Facilities     | Access control, staff/student ID cards   |
| Library        | SSO, digital library cards, federated access |
| Internet/Web   | SSO, MFA, directory services             |
| Student Portal | SSO, digital credentials, profile management |
| Research       | Federated identity, access management    |
| HR/Payroll     | Directory services, SSO, MFA             |
| Guest Access   | Temporary credentials, self-service      |
